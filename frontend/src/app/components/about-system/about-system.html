Our Mission

To build a smart, automated system that instantly connects disaster victims with the right NGOs. By using location mapping and monitoring real-time capacity, we aim to speed up aid delivery and improve disaster management.

Our Vision

To lead the future of disaster response, where smart dispatching replaces manual chaos. We envision a secure digital platform where no aid is lost, no NGO is overloaded, and every affected citizen receives help quickly, fairly, and transparently.
